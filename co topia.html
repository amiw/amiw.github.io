Co-topia Housing

Diversity architecture provides communal spaces that open opportunities for human interaction. Located in the diverse city of San Francisco, Co-topia is an apartment complex where people of different backgrounds, ethnicities, and ages can meet and share together. The design of Co-topia emphasizes the use of communal spaces but also respects individual privacy. The floors alternate between communal and individual spaces, surrounding the tenants with their neighbors.
